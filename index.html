Sobre

A system identification and data collection for cattle in the corral

This system seeks to lessen problems related to traceability, identification and data collection in bovines, depending on the need and lack of implementation of information systems in the field, aimed at small, medium and large farmers. The project in question aims to demonstrate the application of data collection directly from cattle in the corral, using simple techniques, free software and hardware, for restriction on costs for implanting the system..
